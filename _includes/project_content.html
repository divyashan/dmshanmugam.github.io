Towards Principled Test-Time Augmentation
Test-time augmentation - the pooling of predictions across cropped or transformed versions of a test input to increase accuracy - is a common practice in image classification. We provide an empirical analysis of test-time augmentation across 15 architectures on ImageNet. Further, we introduce a learning-based test-time augmentation method that significantly outperforms existing approaches. Under review, CVPR 2020.
